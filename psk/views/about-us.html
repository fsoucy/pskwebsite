About Phi Sig

Phi Sig offers a change of pace from the rigors of MIT. We have some of the most driven students, athletes, and leaders the Institute has to offer. Located in the heart of Kenmore Square since 1902, Phi Sig is a pillar of the social scene for college students in Boston. An entrepreneurial culture gives brothers the opportunity and motivation to realize new interests and pursue well-established passions. Brothers go on to become leaders in their industries, start businesses, and further their studies in top graduate and medical schools. Since our founding in 1873, we firmly stand behind our cardinal principles of brotherhood, scholarship, and character.

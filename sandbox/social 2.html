Social Philosophy

At Phi Sig, we strive to do what we can to praise the academic and athletic achievements of our brothers but we also recognize the importance of social interaction. The ability to befriend and enjoy the company of people of many different backgrounds is something that sets Phi Sigs apart. Our location in Kenmore Square allows us to easily come into contact with students at Boston University, Northeastern, Berkeley and several other local colleges.

Events

Our social chairs arrange a number of events throughout the semester with the goal of facilitating this social interaction. During a typical semester, our house will typically host mixers with local sororities and societies, house parties, our Iron Chef week, and, of course, a semester formal. For the spring semester, we also host a Marathon Day Celebration to support runners passing in front of our house.


If you would like to arrange an event with the brothers of Phi Sigma Kappa send and email to our social chairs.
